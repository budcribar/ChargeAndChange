Benefits of Battery Electric Vehicles

Cheaper to Drive

Electrons are about five times cheaper than gasoline!

More Fun

Instant torque provides unbeatable acceleration

More Convenient

Plug your car in at home instead of driving to a gas station and pumping gas

Cleaner

No tailpipe emissions and much less brake dust

Safer

Electric cars typically have a lower center of gravity and more evenly distributed weight which makes winter driving safer. They and have larger crumple zones and don't catch fire as often.

Quieter

Internal Combustion cars are typically twice as loud as an electric

More Perks

Tax Credits, free charging, prime parking locations
